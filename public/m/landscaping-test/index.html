About Us
Welcome to
Green Valley Landscaping

For over a decade, Green Valley Landscaping has been transforming outdoor spaces across Portland, OR into something extraordinary. We believe that great landscaping isn't just about plants and pavers — it's about creating environments where life happens.

Our team of certified designers and master craftsmen bring deep knowledge of Portland's unique climate, native plants, and soil conditions to every project. From intimate courtyard gardens to sprawling estate grounds, we approach each space with fresh eyes and proven expertise.
